Undergraduate Research Assistant, McSCert (Summer 2024)

During the summer of 2024, I participated in a CO-OP position as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at McSCert. In this role, I was involved in a collaborative project with an industrial partner. Our primary task was to work with a sample of industrial test cases provided in XML format. Using generative AI, we transformed the natural language descriptions from these test cases into a formal, code-like structure. We then compared the AI-generated outputs to the original implementations to evaluate their accuracy and effectiveness.
